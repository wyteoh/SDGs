Community Participation Examples

Community Collaboration:

Urban Farms: Transform unused urban spaces into community farms for shared fresh produce, reducing transportation and distribution costs.

Composting Projects: Establish community composting stations to convert food waste into fertilizer for local use.


School Involvement:

Schools implement "Zero-Waste Lunch" programs, educating students on avoiding food waste while sourcing ingredients from local farms.



Corporate Actions:

Restaurants offer smaller portion options, encouraging diners to order only what they need.

Retailers sell "imperfect" fruits and vegetables at discounts, avoiding waste due to aesthetic standards.
